Istanbul Nonlinear Dynamics & Integrability Workshop 2025

 

It is our pleasure to invite you to attend Istanbul Nonlinear Dynamics & Integrability Workshop 2025 in Istanbul, Türkiye.

This workshop will take place at Faculty of Chemical and Metallurgical Engineering, Department of Mathematical Engineering, Yıldız Technical University, Türkiye between October 30-31, 2025.

The aim of this meeting is to bring researchers in nonlinear dynamical systems and integrable systems to give talks or present posters on new results.

You may find the workshop poster here.

The workshop is supported by TÜBİTAK (under Project No: 123N954).
